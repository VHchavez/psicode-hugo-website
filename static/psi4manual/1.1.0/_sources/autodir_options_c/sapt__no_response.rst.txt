:term:`NO_RESPONSE <NO_RESPONSE (SAPT)>`
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      Don't solve the CPHF equations? Evaluate :math:`E_{ind}^{(20)}` and :math:`E_{exch-ind}^{(20)}` instead of their response-including counterparts. Only turn on this option if the induction energy is not going to be used.

      * **Type**: :ref:`boolean <op_c_boolean>`
      * **Default**: false
