:term:`MRCC_METHOD <MRCC_METHOD (MRCC)>`
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""

      If more than one root is requested and calc=1, LR-CC (EOM-CC) calculation is performed automatically for the excited states. This overrides all automatic determination of method and will only work with :py:func:`~driver.energy`.
